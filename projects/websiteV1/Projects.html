Please read this for [Project 2 - CNG]!



Note: CNG was created for a specific environment. Employer requires us to use Citrix(form of virtual desktop) that has strict limits on various aspects.


These are some questions you may have on CNG:

  • Why is it best suitable for Internet Explorer browser?
  • Why is it one big HTML file?
  • Why didn't you make it as a website instead of distributing as HTML file?
  • Why are some of your links not working?

All the questions above can be answered by these:

  • Work PC only allowed Internet Explorer.
  • All the websites were blocked. (reason why I could only distribute it as ONE .html file)
  • Most links only work within Citrix - job specific environment.
  • Many other reasons that invovles system limitations.

Why did you create this project and how did it benefit your team?

CNG was created to increase agent's productivity. In fact, using CNG increased team's productivity by whopping 50% compared to traditional note creator. This was officially tested by team managers with groups of employees.

How come the employer didn't officially approve your project?

It was first approved to be used for our site. And I was suppose to have a meeting with contracted client to talk about this project to become official for all sites. But then all of a sudden, COVID-19 provoked. This created internal chaos as our department went through donwsizing, transitioning to work-from-home, re-structuring pay system, creating/terminating new projects, etc. My head operation manager who was vouching my project was a victim of downsizing as well. Also I was placed in IT department temporarily, which made harder for me to stay updated on our protocol changes. With all these chaos happening, it seemed like my proposal was forgotten as now the company has to focus on many different projects as well. However, many agents from our site is still utilizing CNG.


"I spent a lot of time on this project. Trust me, this was the best thing I could come up with in that environment. Well at least from my ability." - Me, 2020
